Our Background

BUILT TO COMPETE

Team Hyperion is a passionate group of high school students from Brisbane Boys' College, located in Queensland, Australia. After a successful 1.5 years in the Lightweight division, we were proud to represent Australia at the 2025 RoboCup Junior International Competition in Salvador, Brazil.

In Salvador, we achieved outstanding results — placing 14th individually and earning 1st place in the Lightweight SuperTeam division. We then returned home and remained undefeated at the 2025 Australian National Competition, reclaiming Australia's qualification spot for 2026.

We are now preparing to represent our school, our community, and our country at the 2026 RoboCup Junior International Competition in Incheon, South Korea — our most ambitious challenge yet.
