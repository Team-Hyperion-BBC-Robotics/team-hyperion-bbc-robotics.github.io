Adafruit BNO055 - Absolute Orientation IMU

The BNO055 features an onboard Bosch processor that handles sensor fusion, combining accelerometer, gyroscope, and magnetometer data into stable quaternions and Euler angles.

A high-performance, brushed DC motor with one of the highest power densities in its size class. The precious-metal-brushed design is ideal for low-current, high-efficiency applications.
